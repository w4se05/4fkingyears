> [!Definition]
> A random variable whose values is 0 and 1 is called Bernoulli random variable.
> 

Given random variable $X$ with the probability of 1 is $p$
$(0\leq p\leq{1})$

We have:
$$E(X)=0\times(1-p)+1\times p=p$$
$$Var(X)=E(X^2)-E(X)^2=p-p^2=p\,(1-p)$$
