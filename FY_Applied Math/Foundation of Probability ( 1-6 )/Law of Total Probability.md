# We have: 
## $P(B) = P(A_1 \cap B) + P(A_2 \cap B) + \ldots + P(A_n \cap B)$ 
# and

## $P(A_i \cap B) = P(A_i)P(B|A_i)$ ( [[Conditional Probability]])

# Therefore: 
# $P(B) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} P(A_i)P(B|A_i)$
